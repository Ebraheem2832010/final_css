Several different methods must be used to identify the patient in order to properly deal with him

  • As in giving him any medicine

  • or taking blood samples

  • or any other procedure




















It requires medical staff

  • Confirm the identity of the correct patient or that they are dealing with the correct patient

  • All treatments and interventions are directed at that patient.
